About

sss_election_model is a sorta non-serious election model developed for the 2020 U.S. presidential election. You can read more about how our model works here.

This was a project from the group hacking-the-election. Hacking The Election is a politics-centric research group that works on engaging and groundbreaking programming projects. You can see other projects from Hacking The Election on our github.
